Describe what you want and the AI builds a complete meal plan — drawing from your recipes, curated dinners, and pantry.

Tips for a great meal plan click to expand
Mention any of these in your request:
  • How many meals — e.g. "5 dinners and 2 lunches"
  • Special occasions — e.g. "one fancy dinner for Saturday"
  • Ingredients to use up — e.g. "use the chicken in my pantry"
  • Cuisine preferences — e.g. "mostly Italian and Asian"
  • Budget or effort — e.g. "quick weeknight meals, nothing over 45 min"
  • Variety rules — e.g. "no red meat two days in a row"
Example prompts:
Simple weekly plan: "5 dinners for the week, mix of meat and vegetarian, nothing too complicated."
Pantry-first + special occasion: "Plan 4 dinners that use up what's in my pantry. Include one vegetarian and something special for Saturday."
Full week with variety: "Full week: 5 dinners, 3 lunches, and 2 snacks. Prefer Asian and Mediterranean flavors. Don't repeat the same protein two days in a row."
Budget & time-conscious: "4 quick dinners (under 30 min), budget ingredients, one must have fish. Use pantry items where possible."
